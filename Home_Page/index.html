About WeatherCast

Welcome to our WeatherCast website! We are committed to providing you with accurate and up-to-date weather information of Telanagana to help you plan your day, week, or even month ahead. Our team of meteorologists and weather enthusiasts work tirelessly to gather data from reliable sources and use advanced forecasting techniques to provide you with the most accurate weather predictions.

WeatherCast is a web app which has a Machine Learning model running at the back. The purpose of developing this app is to predict the Weather of Telangana Districts of Year 2023. This model is based on the Weather Prediction in Telangana dataset of Year 2018-2022. This project was given be T-AIM by NASSCOM for Graduate Students (2023,2024,2025) WEBSITE_Link. The dataset of Telangana was provided by Open Data Telangana DATASET_Link. At our WeatherCast website, we understand the importance of having reliable weather information at your fingertips. We strive to keep our website user-friendly and accessible to everyone. Whether you're a professional meteorologist or just someone looking to plan your day, we hope you find our website useful and informative.

Thank you for choosing our WeatherCast website!

Limitation Of WeatherCast

Indeed, weather forecasting apps have limitations, and one of them is that the predicted values are limited to a certain period, in this case, up to 2023. This is because weather forecasting is a complex task that relies on various factors, such as historical data, satellite images, and weather models.

Additionally, some data may not be available or reliable enough to make accurate predictions. For example, if a weather station is not functioning, or if there are gaps in historical data, it can affect the accuracy of the forecasts.

However, meteorologists and weather experts are constantly working to improve weather forecasting models and methods, which could lead to more accurate predictions in the future.

The WeatherCast is LIMITED to some district because of unavailable past data of " Bhadradri-Kothagudem Jayashankar-Bhupalpally , Jogulamba-Gadwal , Komaram Bheem-Asifabad , Kumuram Bheem - Asifabad , Medchal-Malkajgiri , Mulug , Narayanpet , Rajanna-Siricilla , Rangareddy , Warangal (R) , Warangal (U) , Yadadri-Bhongir " of these Districts.

How To Use

  1. Click on Predictor Button at 1st View of Page .
  2. Enter location of Telangana Districts.
  3. Select the Date(till Dec-2023) of weather information you want to see.
  4. View the weather forecast for your location of particular Date.
  5. You can see all the information on result page.
